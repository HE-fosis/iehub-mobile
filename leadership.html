Lessons in Leadership

A strong leader is the cornerstone of any effective team. True leadership in an ISoc is about serving your people and the Muslim community with humility and sincerity.

QUESTION

What do you expect from your team?

ANSWER

Prompt task completion, upholding Islamic values & consistent attendance. Empowering individuals with well-defined roles streamlines work.

QUESTION

How do we improve our communication?

ANSWER

With effective communication techniques from the Sunnah. Learn how to convey the message of Islam clearly & compassionately.

Lead by Example

Focused • Efficient • Creative
