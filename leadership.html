The Golden Circle

WHY

To serve & uplift Muslim students for the sake of Allah.

Planning with sincerity, teamwork, and communication.

Through high-quality events, socials, and dawah activities.

Lessons in Leadership

True leadership in an ISoc is about serving your people and the Muslim community with humility and sincerity.

QUESTION

What do you expect from your team?

ANSWER

Prompt task completion, values & consistent attendance. Empowering individuals with well-defined roles streamlines work.

QUESTION

How do we improve communication?

ANSWER

With effective communication techniques from the Sunnah. Convey the message of Islam clearly & compassionately.

Lead by Example

+

Serve with humility and sincerity. Lead with genuine care and inspire through your actions.

Communicate with Clarity

+

Clear and consistent communication is vital. Ensure info flows freely and feedback is encouraged.

Defining Roles

+

Defining clear roles: everyone knows their responsibilities and where decisions are made.
