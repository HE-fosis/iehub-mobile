WHAT IS

Tarbiyyah?

Rooted in the Prophetic legacy, Tarbiyyah is the intentional cultivation of the self to achieve excellence in character.

📖 Knowledge
⚖️ Iman
💎 Akhlaq
🌙 Discipline

Knowledge

Building a resilient intellectual foundation through the study of the Qur’an and Sunnah.

Foundational Stage

Iman & Taqwa

Strengthening spiritual resilience and mindfulness to remain grounded under pressure.

Internalisation Stage

Akhlaq (Character)

Developing prophetic manners (Adab) in every interaction. This is the heart of Tarbiyyah and the proof of internal refinement.

The Core Pillar

Discipline

Building consistency through Salah and Self-reflection to stay aligned with your purpose.
