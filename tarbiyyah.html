WHAT IS

Tarbiyyah?

Rooted in the Prophetic legacy, Tarbiyyah is the intentional cultivation of the self to achieve excellence in character.

Level 01

Seeking Knowledge (Ilm)

The first step of tarbiyyah is seeking knowledge that nourishes the soul and transforms how we think and act.

What to Reflect On:
The Pillars:

Revisit the foundations of belief and action — the 5 pillars of Islam and 6 pillars of Imaan.

Time as an Amanah:

Reflect on Surah Al-ʿAsr. Every minute is a trust. Avoid procrastination.

Prophetic Leadership:

Study the empathy and humility of the Prophet ﷺ — essential for responsibility.

Foundation Snapshots

•
Creed & Purpose

Surah al-Asr: Time as an Amanat and the cure for procrastination.

•
Daily Practise

Refining the fundamentals: Wudhu, Salah, and the 5 Pillars.

•
Prophetic Character

Empathy, Honesty (integrity in ISoc), and Justice (fair feedback).

Level 02

The Heart (Tazkiyah)

Character is built by aligning intentions to please Allah, allowing hearts to find true rest.

What to Reflect On:
Tawakkul:

True trust is taking courageous action while accepting Allah’s decree.

Ihsaan:

Strive for excellence (worshipping Allah as though you see Him) without falling into guilt.

Niyyah (Intention):

Every email or event is an act of worship if done solely for Allah’s sake.

Purification Principles

•
Antidote to Anxiety

Practising Tawakkul: Reliance on Allah is courageous action.

•
Combating Perfectionism

Pursuing Ihsan while avoiding Arrogance and guilt.

•
Intention Setting

Renewing Niyyah: "My intention is for the sake of Allah".

Level 03

Practice (Khidmah)

Putting knowledge into sincere action. Leading through compassion and servant leadership.

What to Reflect On:
Service as Worship:

Admin is ibadah. Use your degree as Sadaqah Jariyah.

Accountability:

Reflect daily: "Did I serve with sincerity today?"

Community:

Seek opportunities to serve through FOSIS or student projects.

COMMITTEE GOVERNANCE

Conduct

Modesty in gender interactions and polite language are required. Professionalism is its own form of Da'wah.

WARNING: NAJWA

Secret cliques breed suspicion. Transparency is non-negotiable.

Barakah

  • Start with Qur'an and Du'a.
  • Maintain minutes and agendas.
  • Rotate Jumu'ah reminders.

Sustainability
